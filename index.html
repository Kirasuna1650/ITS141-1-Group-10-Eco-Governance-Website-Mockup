Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Integration

The Eco-Governance platform bridges interface design with clean data tracking, directly serving key UN Sustainable Development Goals in the Metro Manila area:

SDG 3 Good Health & Well-being

Provides real-time, simplified air health indicators (AQI and PM2.5 levels) directly translating complex emission data into practical public health advisory guidelines for vulnerable demographic groups.

SDG 11 Sustainable Cities

Equips Local Government Units (LGUs) with clear carbon-density spatial analytics to support localized traffic restructuring, urban tree-canopy planning, and environmental safety regulations.

SDG 13 Climate Action

Offers a continuous reporting framework aligning with the Climate Change Act of 2009, highlighting high-carbon clusters to formulate data-driven greenhouse gas reduction policies.

SDG 15 Life on Land

Tracks environmental degradation index to assess air pollutant deposition trends and support long-term regional ecological biodiversity preservation actions.
